About the Professor

Ada Lovelace

Ada Lovelace was an English mathematician and writer. She was born in London on December 10, 1815. Through her friendship with "the father of computers" Charles Baggage, she is known for her work on publishing the first algorithm on Baggage's Analytical Engine, making her one of the first computer programmers. She passed away on November 27, 1852.
